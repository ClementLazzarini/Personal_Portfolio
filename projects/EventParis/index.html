Discover Paris

Make the most of your stay in the French capital. Here are a few must-see spots around the city!

Eiffel Tower

The iconic symbol of Paris. Don't miss the sparkling lights at the top of every hour after sunset.

📍 Champ de Mars, 75007 Paris

⏱️ Suggested visit: 2-3 hours

Louvre Museum

The world's largest art museum and a historic monument. Home to the legendary Mona Lisa.

📍 Rue de Rivoli, 75001 Paris

⏱️ Suggested visit: 3-4 hours

The Panthéon

A magnificent monument honoring French history. It houses Foucault's famous pendulum proving Earth's rotation.

📍 Place du Panthéon, 75005 Paris

⏱️ Suggested visit: 1.5 hours

🇫🇷 French Survival Kit

A few tips for your stay in Paris

👋

The Golden Rule: "Bonjour"

In France, always say "Bonjour" (bohn-zhoor) when entering a shop, a restaurant, or before asking a question. It is the absolute key to French politeness!

💶

Tipping: Service Included

Good news! In all French restaurants and cafes, a 15% service charge is automatically included in the price by law ("Service compris"). Tipping extra is entirely optional.

💧

Free Tap Water

Tap water is perfectly safe to drink and free of charge. At a restaurant, simply ask for "Une carafe d'eau s'il vous plaît" instead of paying for bottled water.

☕

Parisian Coffee Protocol

  • Un Café : A single espresso.
  • Un Café Crème : Coffee with hot milk.
  • Un Café Allongé : Espresso diluted with hot water.
🏛️

Fun Fact: The Zinc Roofs

Look up! Over 70% of Paris roofs are made of zinc, giving the city its iconic grey skyline. Installed during the Haussmann renovations in the 19th century, they are currently a candidate for UNESCO World Heritage status.
